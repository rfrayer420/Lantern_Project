# Complete World Bible v1

- Volume I playable areas: The Labyrinth and The Lantern Forest.
- Future regions are expansion content.
- The Forager is the First Remembered and First Heretic.
- The Lantern is an ancient artifact stored beneath the center of the Labyrinth.
- The Chamber of Embers houses the Lantern.
- The Custodian is the High Cenobite who guards the Lantern.
- The Great Hunt begins after The Forager remembers fragments of humanity.
