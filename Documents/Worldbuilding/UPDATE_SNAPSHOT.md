# Lantern Project Update Snapshot

## Newly Established Canon
- The Forager is the First Remembered and First Heretic.
- The Remembered do not remember their human names.
- The Lantern restores fragments of emotional memory only.
- There are no living humans in Hell.
- The Lantern is stored beneath the center of the Labyrinth.
- The Chamber of Embers houses the Lantern.
- The Custodian is the High Cenobite who guards the Lantern.
- Volume I only contains The Labyrinth and The Lantern Forest.
- The Forager Chronicles has not started and will be generated one page at a time.
