# The Drowned Pilgrim

## Classification
Revenant

## Threat Level
III

## Habitat
Flooded passages

## Description
A waterlogged traveler searching for a lost destination.

## Statistics
- HP: 220
- Armor: 3
- Speed: Medium
- Damage: 2d8 drowning grasp

## Abilities
- Summon Floodwater
- Drowning Grasp
- Mist Form

## Weaknesses
- Sunlight

## Loot
- Pilgrim's Coin
- Waterlogged Journal

## Special Dice Rolls
Failed save begins drowning even on dry land.

## Encounter Notes
Use in impossible-water scenes.
