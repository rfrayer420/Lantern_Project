# The Lantern Beast

## Classification
Ancient Guardian

## Threat Level
V+

## Habitat
Deep Labyrinth gates

## Description
An enormous predator glowing with embers and lantern fire.

## Statistics
- HP: 1000
- Armor: 10
- Speed: Fast
- Damage: 5d12 soul rend

## Abilities
- Soul Rend
- Darkness Aura
- Summon Lesser Horrors
- Unbreakable Hide
- Lantern Flame Breath

## Weaknesses
- Lantern Configuration can force submission

## Loot
- Heart of the Labyrinth
- Eternal Ember
- Lantern Fang

## Special Dice Rolls
Each round its aura may extinguish weak lights.

## Encounter Notes
Use as an ultimate guardian.
