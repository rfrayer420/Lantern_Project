# The Children in the Fog

## Classification
Collective Spirit

## Threat Level
III

## Habitat
Fog banks and nursery halls

## Description
Pale children who lure travelers deeper into the Labyrinth.

## Statistics
- HP: 180 collective
- Armor: 2
- Speed: Fast
- Damage: 2d6 psychic

## Abilities
- Lure the Lost
- Shared Weeping
- Mist Vanish
- Memory Theft

## Weaknesses
- Speaking their names and refusing to follow

## Loot
- Lost Toy
- Glass Marble

## Special Dice Rolls
Following them moves party to worse location.

## Encounter Notes
Use carefully for eerie misdirection.
