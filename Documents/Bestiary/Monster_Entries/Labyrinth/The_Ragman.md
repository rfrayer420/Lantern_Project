# The Ragman

## Classification
Stalker

## Threat Level
III

## Habitat
Dark sleeping chambers

## Description
A thin figure in rags that replaces memories with nightmares.

## Statistics
- HP: 180
- Armor: 2
- Speed: Fast
- Damage: 2d8 nightmare touch

## Abilities
- Nightmare Touch
- Shadow Step
- Sleep Curse

## Weaknesses
- Lantern light reveals true form

## Loot
- Tattered Memory
- Dream Cloth

## Special Dice Rolls
Failed save causes nightmares that prevent rest.

## Encounter Notes
Use as long-haunt enemy.
