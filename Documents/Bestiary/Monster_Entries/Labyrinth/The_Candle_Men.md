# The Candle Men

## Classification
Stalker

## Threat Level
III

## Habitat
Dark paths

## Description
Burned humanoids carrying candles in their skulls.

## Statistics
- HP: 150
- Armor: 3
- Speed: Medium
- Damage: 2d6 fire

## Abilities
- False Guidance
- Extinguish Hope
- Ignite Fear

## Weaknesses
- Running water extinguishes flames

## Loot
- Soul Wax
- Candle Skull

## Special Dice Rolls
Failed roll follows their false light for 1d4 turns.

## Encounter Notes
Use as deceptive guides.
