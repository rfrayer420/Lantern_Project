# The Last Penitent

## Classification
Eternal Prisoner

## Threat Level
V

## Habitat
Penance halls

## Description
A chained figure bearing every sin engraved into flesh.

## Statistics
- HP: 480
- Armor: 6
- Speed: Slow
- Damage: 4d8 chain shockwave

## Abilities
- Guilt Projection
- Burden of Sins
- Regeneration Through Suffering

## Weaknesses
- Genuine absolution

## Loot
- Penitent's Chain
- Tablet of Confession

## Special Dice Rolls
Damage dealt to it may reflect guilt damage.

## Encounter Notes
Use as tragic boss.
