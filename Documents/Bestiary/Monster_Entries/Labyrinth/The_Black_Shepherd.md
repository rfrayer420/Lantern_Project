# The Black Shepherd

## Classification
Herdmaster

## Threat Level
IV

## Habitat
Endless pastures of black stone

## Description
A shadowy figure leading eyeless sheep.

## Statistics
- HP: 320
- Armor: 4
- Speed: Medium
- Damage: 3d8 crook strike

## Abilities
- Summon Flock
- Curse of Wandering
- Path Erasure

## Weaknesses
- Bells and pure lantern light

## Loot
- Shepherd's Crook
- Black Wool

## Special Dice Rolls
Failed save causes party to lose mapped path.

## Encounter Notes
Use to disorient travelers.
