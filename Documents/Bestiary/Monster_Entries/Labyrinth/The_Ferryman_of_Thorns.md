# The Ferryman of Thorns

## Classification
Guide/Executioner

## Threat Level
IV

## Statistics

- Armor: 4
- Speed: Medium
- Damage: 3d8 thorn lash

## Abilities

- Demand Toll
- Summon Drowned Souls

## Weaknesses

## Loot

- Thornwood Oar
