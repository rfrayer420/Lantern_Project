# The Maw in the Walls

## Classification
Environmental Horror

## Threat Level
V+

## Habitat
Living stone corridors

## Description
The Labyrinth develops mouths within its walls.

## Statistics
- HP: 700
- Armor: 8
- Speed: Environmental
- Damage: 5d10 bite

## Abilities
- Consume Flesh
- Swallow Rooms
- Mimic Voices
- False Passages

## Weaknesses
- Lantern Configuration temporarily closes mouths

## Loot
- Teeth of the Labyrinth
- Stone Heart Fragment

## Special Dice Rolls
Room may shift or vanish each round.

## Encounter Notes
Use as a boss-level hazard.
