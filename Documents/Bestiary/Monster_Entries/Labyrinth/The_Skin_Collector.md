# The Skin Collector

## Classification
Hunter

## Threat Level
V

## Habitat
Trophy halls

## Description
A towering creature wearing stitched skins of victims.

## Statistics
- HP: 450
- Armor: 6
- Speed: Medium
- Damage: 3d10 hooks

## Abilities
- Mimic Appearance
- Fear Aura
- Harvest Identity

## Weaknesses
- Fire destroys stolen skins

## Loot
- Collector's Needle
- Face of Remembrance

## Special Dice Rolls
Failed save lets it mimic the target's face.

## Encounter Notes
Use as identity horror.
