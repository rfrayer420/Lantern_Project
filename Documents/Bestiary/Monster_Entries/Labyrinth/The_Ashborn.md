# The Ashborn

## Classification
Revenant

## Threat Level
IV

## Habitat
Burned corridors

## Description
Burned souls that crumble into ash and reform.

## Statistics
- HP: 300
- Armor: 4
- Speed: Medium
- Damage: 3d8 fire

## Abilities
- Reform from Ash
- Ember Storm
- Smoke Form

## Weaknesses
- Blessed water prevents regeneration

## Loot
- Ashen Core
- Ember Shard

## Special Dice Rolls
On defeat roll d6; on 4+ reforms unless weakness used.

## Encounter Notes
Use in burned maze sections.
