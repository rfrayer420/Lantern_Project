# The Weeper

## Classification
Spirit

## Threat Level
IV

## Habitat
Children's rooms and forgotten homes

## Description
A grieving mother spirit searching forever for a lost child.

## Statistics
- HP: 250
- Armor: Ethereal
- Speed: Medium
- Damage: 3d8 sorrow

## Abilities
- Summon Lost Children
- Emotional Collapse
- Possession

## Weaknesses
- Returning a lost possession

## Loot
- Mother's Locket
- Tearstone

## Special Dice Rolls
Failed save causes helpless grief for 1 round.

## Encounter Notes
Use for tragedy-heavy encounters.
