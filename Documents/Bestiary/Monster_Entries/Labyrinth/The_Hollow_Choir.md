# The Hollow Choir

## Classification
Collective Entity

## Threat Level
III

## Habitat
Ruined cathedrals

## Description
Faceless robed beings that sing truths people hide.

## Statistics
- HP: 200
- Armor: 3
- Speed: Slow
- Damage: 2d8 psychic

## Abilities
- Song of Madness
- Shared Consciousness
- Echo of Guilt

## Weaknesses
- Silence
- breaking three singers

## Loot
- Echo Bone
- Choir Veil

## Special Dice Rolls
Failed Wisdom save causes frightened condition.

## Encounter Notes
Use for confession and guilt scenes.
