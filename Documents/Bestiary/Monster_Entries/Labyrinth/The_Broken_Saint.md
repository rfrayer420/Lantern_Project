# The Broken Saint

## Classification
Fallen Celestial

## Threat Level
V

## Habitat
Chained chapels

## Description
An angelic being shattered by the Labyrinth, halo broken and wings chained.

## Statistics
- HP: 500
- Armor: 6
- Speed: Medium fly/hover
- Damage: 4d8 radiant agony

## Abilities
- Radiant Agony
- Chains of Penance
- Judgment Gaze
- One Resurrection

## Weaknesses
- Speaking its forgotten name

## Loot
- Halo Fragment
- Feather of Grace

## Special Dice Rolls
First death triggers resurrection unless named.

## Encounter Notes
Use as tragic high-level foe.
