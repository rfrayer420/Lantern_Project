# The Bell Keeper

## Classification
Warden

## Threat Level
IV

## Habitat
Bell towers and metal halls

## Description
A gaunt figure carrying a bronze bell whose toll summons horrors.

## Statistics
- HP: 300
- Armor: 5
- Speed: Slow
- Damage: 3d8 sonic

## Abilities
- Toll of Fear
- Summon Lesser Monsters
- Deafening Wave

## Weaknesses
- Silence
- shattering the bell

## Loot
- Bell Clapper
- Bronze Shard

## Special Dice Rolls
Each bell toll may summon a lesser monster.

## Encounter Notes
Use as encounter escalator.
