# The Bone Orchard

## Classification
Living Environment

## Threat Level
IV

## Habitat
Bone groves

## Description
Trees grown from the bones of the dead.

## Statistics
- HP: 350
- Armor: 5
- Speed: Immobile
- Damage: 3d8 bone spears

## Abilities
- Animate Skeletons
- Bone Spear Barrage
- Root Entanglement

## Weaknesses
- Fire and salt

## Loot
- Bone Fruit
- Orchard Seed

## Special Dice Rolls
Each corpse nearby may become a skeleton.

## Encounter Notes
Use as a battlefield/environment.
