# Moss Stalker

## Classification
Forest Predator

## Threat Level
II

## Habitat
Moss-covered ruins and dead groves

## Description
A deer-like predator covered in moss, fungus, and hanging vines. It remains motionless for days, waiting for prey.

## Statistics
- HP: 80
- Armor: 2
- Speed: Medium
- Damage: 1d8 claw

## Abilities
- Camouflage
- Ambush Strike
- Spore Burst

## Weaknesses
- Fire
- forced movement out of heavy moss

## Loot
- Moss Antlers
- Spore Sac

## Special Dice Rolls
Natural 20 on its attack infects target with spores until treated.

## Encounter Notes
Use it as a silent ambush predator in overgrown ruins.
