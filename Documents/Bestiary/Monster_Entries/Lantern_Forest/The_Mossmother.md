# The Mossmother

## Classification
Apex Forest Entity

## Threat Level
V

## Habitat
Heart of the Lantern Forest

## Description
An enormous ancient entity that births lesser creatures from her moss-covered body.

## Statistics
- HP: 600
- Armor: 8
- Speed: Slow
- Damage: 4d10 crushing limb

## Abilities
- Birth Spawn
- Forest Command
- Regeneration
- Spore Storm

## Weaknesses
- Lantern flame at her heart-root
- severing sacred roots

## Loot
- Mossmother Heartroot
- Ancient Spore Crown

## Special Dice Rolls
Each round roll d6: on 5-6 she births a lesser creature.

## Encounter Notes
Use as a major boss or regional power.
