# Hollow Husk

## Classification
Undead

## Threat Level
II

## Habitat
Abandoned cabins and dead camps

## Description
An animated corpse filled with roots, insects, and fungal growth.

## Statistics
- HP: 100
- Armor: 1
- Speed: Slow
- Damage: 1d8 claw

## Abilities
- Summon Insects
- Rotting Touch
- Corpse Collapse

## Weaknesses
- Holy fire
- removing the root heart

## Loot
- Seed of Decay

## Special Dice Rolls
Failed Constitution roll causes crawling sickness for 24 hours.

## Encounter Notes
Use as a warning that the forest reclaims bodies.
