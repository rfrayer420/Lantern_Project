# Bog Raider

## Classification
Drowned Warrior

## Threat Level
III

## Habitat
Swamp ruins

## Description
A drowned warrior risen from old swamp battles, still hunting treasure.

## Statistics
- HP: 210
- Armor: 5
- Speed: Medium
- Damage: 2d8 rusted blade

## Abilities
- Drowning Grip
- Rust Blade
- Treasure Curse

## Weaknesses
- Sun-dried earth
- returning stolen treasure

## Loot
- Rust Coin
- Swamp Blade

## Special Dice Rolls
Greedy characters suffer disadvantage resisting its curse.

## Encounter Notes
Use around sunken ruins.
