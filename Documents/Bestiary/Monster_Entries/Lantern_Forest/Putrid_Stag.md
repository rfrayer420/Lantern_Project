# Putrid Stag

## Classification
Undead Beast

## Threat Level
III

## Habitat
Diseased groves

## Description
A rotting elk crowned with broken antlers and green corpse-light eyes.

## Statistics
- HP: 190
- Armor: 4
- Speed: Fast
- Damage: 2d8 antler gore

## Abilities
- Rotting Charge
- Disease Aura
- Corpse Fog

## Weaknesses
- Cleansing flame
- silvered weapons

## Loot
- Putrid Antler
- Rot Eye

## Special Dice Rolls
Failed Constitution roll inflicts forest rot.

## Encounter Notes
Use as a corrupted noble beast.
