# Rootmaw

## Classification
Plant Horror

## Threat Level
III

## Habitat
Deep forest sinkholes

## Description
A gigantic root system with a cavernous maw lined with wooden teeth.

## Statistics
- HP: 180
- Armor: 4
- Speed: Slow
- Damage: 2d8 bite

## Abilities
- Root Snare
- Swallow Whole
- Regeneration

## Weaknesses
- Axes
- fire
- severing feeder roots

## Loot
- Heartwood Core
- Root Teeth

## Special Dice Rolls
Failed escape roll by 5+ means the target is partially buried.

## Encounter Notes
Best used as a terrain monster rather than a wandering beast.
