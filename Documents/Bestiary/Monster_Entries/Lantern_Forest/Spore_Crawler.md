# Spore Crawler

## Classification
Fungal Vermin

## Threat Level
II

## Habitat
Rotting logs and mushroom caves

## Description
A centipede-like horror made of fungus, roots, and chitinous bark.

## Statistics
- HP: 90
- Armor: 2
- Speed: Medium
- Damage: 1d8 bite plus spores

## Abilities
- Poison Spores
- Wall Crawl
- Burrow into Rot

## Weaknesses
- Dry heat
- salt

## Loot
- Toxic Spore Gland
- Chitin Bark

## Special Dice Rolls
Entering its spore cloud requires a save or coughing fit.

## Encounter Notes
Good for tunnels and claustrophobic scenes.
