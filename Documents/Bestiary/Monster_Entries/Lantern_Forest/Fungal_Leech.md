# Fungal Leech

## Classification
Corpse Parasite

## Threat Level
III

## Habitat
Wet corpse piles and swamp roots

## Description
A segmented parasite that burrows into corpses and controls them.

## Statistics
- HP: 140
- Armor: 2
- Speed: Medium
- Damage: 1d10 bite

## Abilities
- Corpse Puppet
- Blood Drain
- Burrow

## Weaknesses
- Salt
- removing host head

## Loot
- Leech Pearl
- Parasite Segment

## Special Dice Rolls
If a corpse is nearby, it may enter a new host on defeat.

## Encounter Notes
Use for body-horror reveals.
