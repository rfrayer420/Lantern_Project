# Lantern Wisp

## Classification
Spirit

## Threat Level
I

## Habitat
Foggy paths and old trails

## Description
Floating blue-amber lights that lure travelers off safe paths.

## Statistics
- HP: 30
- Armor: 0
- Speed: Fast
- Damage: 1d4 psychic

## Abilities
- False Path
- Fear Aura
- Vanish in Fog

## Weaknesses
- True lantern light
- spoken names of the dead

## Loot
- Wisp Essence

## Special Dice Rolls
Failed Wisdom roll causes the traveler to walk in the wrong direction for 1d6 turns.

## Encounter Notes
Use in groups to split parties.
