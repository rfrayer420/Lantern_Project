# Rotbloom

## Classification
Carnivorous Plant

## Threat Level
III

## Habitat
Old graves and shaded gullies

## Description
A person-sized flower that mimics human cries to lure prey.

## Statistics
- HP: 160
- Armor: 3
- Speed: Immobile
- Damage: 2d8 bite

## Abilities
- Mimic Cry
- Acid Nectar
- Vine Drag

## Weaknesses
- Cutting the central stem
- cold

## Loot
- Rotbloom Nectar
- Petal Blades

## Special Dice Rolls
Failed perception check mistakes it for a crying human.

## Encounter Notes
Use for rescue-bait horror.
