# Crooked Treant

## Classification
Corrupted Guardian

## Threat Level
IV

## Habitat
Ancient groves

## Description
An old tree guardian twisted by corruption and grief.

## Statistics
- HP: 320
- Armor: 6
- Speed: Slow
- Damage: 3d8 branch slam

## Abilities
- Root Prison
- Falling Limb
- Ancient Curse

## Weaknesses
- Axes blessed by lantern flame
- fire at root wounds

## Loot
- Ancient Bark
- Heart Knot

## Special Dice Rolls
Failed save ages wooden gear and weakens shields.

## Encounter Notes
Use as a tragic mini-boss.
