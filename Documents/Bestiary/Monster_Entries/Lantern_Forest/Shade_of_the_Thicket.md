# Shade of the Thicket

## Classification
Shadow Humanoid

## Threat Level
IV

## Habitat
Dense black woods

## Description
A thin shadowy humanoid that moves between trees and steals voices.

## Statistics
- HP: 240
- Armor: 3
- Speed: Fast
- Damage: 2d8 shadow touch

## Abilities
- Voice Theft
- Tree Step
- Fear Whisper

## Weaknesses
- Lantern light
- speaking its stolen voice back

## Loot
- Black Leaf
- Stolen Whisper

## Special Dice Rolls
Failed save prevents speech for 1d6 rounds.

## Encounter Notes
Use as stalking psychological horror.
