# Thornling

## Classification
Forest Spirit Swarm

## Threat Level
I

## Habitat
Briar nests and hollow trees

## Description
Small vicious spirits made from bark, thorns, and old malice.

## Statistics
- HP: 25
- Armor: 1
- Speed: Fast
- Damage: 1d4 thorn slash

## Abilities
- Swarm Attack
- Thorn Needles
- Hide in Leaves

## Weaknesses
- Fire
- iron nails

## Loot
- Thorn Heart
- Splinter Charm

## Special Dice Rolls
Three or more Thornlings gain advantage on swarm attacks.

## Encounter Notes
Great low-level swarm enemy.
