# Whisperbark

## Classification
Ancient Tree Spirit

## Threat Level
IV

## Habitat
Oldest forest trees

## Description
A tree spirit that knows every secret spoken in the forest and mimics any voice.

## Statistics
- HP: 280
- Armor: 5
- Speed: Slow
- Damage: 2d10 branch crush

## Abilities
- Voice Mimic
- Secret Curse
- Root Binding

## Weaknesses
- Truth freely confessed
- cutting the whisper knot

## Loot
- Whisper Knot
- Secret Bark

## Special Dice Rolls
Failed Wisdom roll forces target to reveal a secret.

## Encounter Notes
Use for moral encounters.
