# Briarback

## Classification
Beast

## Threat Level
III

## Habitat
Thorn fields and bramble tunnels

## Description
A massive boar-like creature covered in razor vines and bark plates.

## Statistics
- HP: 220
- Armor: 5
- Speed: Fast charge
- Damage: 2d10 gore

## Abilities
- Charge
- Thorn Explosion
- Bleeding Wounds

## Weaknesses
- Cold
- cutting away vines

## Loot
- Briar Quills
- Thornhide Plate

## Special Dice Rolls
On a critical charge, target is knocked prone and begins bleeding.

## Encounter Notes
Use as a brute encounter in narrow forest paths.
