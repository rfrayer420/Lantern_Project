# Glowcap Herd

## Classification
Fungal Beast Herd

## Threat Level
II

## Habitat
Bioluminescent clearings

## Description
Deer-sized mushroom creatures whose glowing caps hypnotize travelers.

## Statistics
- HP: 70 each
- Armor: 1
- Speed: Medium
- Damage: 1d6 slam

## Abilities
- Hypnotic Glow
- Stampede
- Spore Trail

## Weaknesses
- Bright sudden light
- loud bells

## Loot
- Glowcap Spores
- Soft Cap Hide

## Special Dice Rolls
Failed Wisdom roll makes target follow the herd.

## Encounter Notes
Use as eerie beauty turning dangerous.
