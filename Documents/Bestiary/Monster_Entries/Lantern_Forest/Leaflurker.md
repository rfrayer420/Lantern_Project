# Leaflurker

## Classification
Ambush Predator

## Threat Level
II

## Habitat
Tall trees and leaf piles

## Description
A thin humanoid covered in leaves and bark that strikes from above.

## Statistics
- HP: 100
- Armor: 2
- Speed: Fast climb
- Damage: 1d10 claw

## Abilities
- Drop Strike
- Leaf Camouflage
- Silent Climb

## Weaknesses
- Open ground
- fire

## Loot
- Leafhide
- Curved Claw

## Special Dice Rolls
Surprise attack deals extra 1d8 damage.

## Encounter Notes
Use in canopy ambushes.
