# Murkmire Slug

## Classification
Swamp Mollusk

## Threat Level
III

## Habitat
Black marshes and muddy trails

## Description
A giant slug whose acidic slime dissolves flesh and armor.

## Statistics
- HP: 200
- Armor: 3
- Speed: Slow
- Damage: 2d8 acid

## Abilities
- Acid Trail
- Armor Melt
- Swallow Mud

## Weaknesses
- Salt
- dry ground

## Loot
- Acid Gland
- Slime Resin

## Special Dice Rolls
Standing in slime causes ongoing acid damage.

## Encounter Notes
Use for environmental hazard combat.
