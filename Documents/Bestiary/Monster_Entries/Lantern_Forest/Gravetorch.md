# Gravetorch

## Classification
Undead Guide

## Threat Level
III

## Habitat
Forgotten graves and cemetery paths

## Description
A corpse carrying a lantern made from its own ribcage.

## Statistics
- HP: 180
- Armor: 3
- Speed: Medium
- Damage: 2d8 necrotic flame

## Abilities
- Deadlight
- Grave Call
- Bone Lantern

## Weaknesses
- Blessed burial rites
- extinguishing rib lantern

## Loot
- Rib Lantern Shard
- Grave Ash

## Special Dice Rolls
Failed save sees dead relatives calling from the dark.

## Encounter Notes
Use as an omen-bearing undead.
